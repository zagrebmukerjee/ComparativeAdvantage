Table Ee376-384 –  Exports and imports of goods and services: 1929–2002
 Sources 
 

1929–1997, U.S. Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA), Survey of Current Business (August 1998, with updates from March 2003). Later updates available at the BEA Internet site.

 
 
 Documentation 
 

All series are presented on a balance of payments (BOP) basis. The BOP basis for goods reflects adjustments for timing, coverage, and valuation to the data provided by the Census Bureau. These adjustments include military trade of U.S. defense agencies, additional nonmonetary gold transactions, and inland freight in Canada and Mexico. Goods valuation are free alongside ship (f.a.s.) for exports and customs value for imports.
